UNDER CONSTRUCTION

In a world where everyone wants to be viewed as a thought leader yet few possess any original thoughts, Improbable Ventures stands apart -- unbowed and unbalanced, on-prem and off kilter, ready to imbue your turgid prose and stale ideas with life, color, and some light seasoning (usually paprika). We turn your nouns into verbs, and your verbs into heat-seeking missiles. We spit on your putrid adjectives and emasculated adverbs. We would never allow your modifiers to dangle. This is our story.
